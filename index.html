Welcome to Thrive 2 Drive Driving School!
Thrive 2 Drive Driving School is an experienced and affordable driving school run by a former RTA/RMS Driver Testing Officer with years of experience across the entire Sydney region.
No matter what confidence or ability level - our qualified driving instructor aims to teach you essential driving skills, so that you are confident to pass your driving test with the focus on vital skills to keep you safe on the road. When you book to drive with Thrive 2 Drive you'll be learning good driving habits from a professional!
Our Services suit all people of all driving experience levels. Specfically we offer:
  • A range of driving lesson packages for a variety of needs.
  • Pre-test assessments to prepare you for your driving test.
  • Reliable automatic vehicle fitted with dual controls.
  • Full insurance cover.
  • Pick-up and drop-off from your location.
  • Friendly, professional and patient Instructor.
  • Log book maintenance for Learner drivers
To book a lesson with Thrive 2 Drive either call on 0400280761 or make an enquiry via our online booking form. You will then receive a booking confirmation by email. We hope that our Frequently Asked Questions page will answer any further questions you might have. But feel free to ask us questions by emailing info@thrive2drive.com.au
